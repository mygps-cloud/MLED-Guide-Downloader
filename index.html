How to guide

When you open the application, you will be taken to the main map page, where you will find the location of all chargers. In order to use the application, you need to be authorized, so when you scan the QR code or log in, you will be authorized.

1

Step 1

To authenticate and use the app, you need to enter your phone number.

2

Step 2

After entering your phone number, you will receive an SMS code, which will allow you to complete the authorization process.

3

Step 3

After you have completed the authorization process and scanned the required connector, you will need to deposit or pay the amount, after which the charging will begin.

4

Step 4

The next step is to choose a recharge package, after which you proceed to deposit or pay.

5

Step 5

You choose the desired amount you want to deposit into your account.

6

Step 6

At this stage, you choose a payment method and complete the payment. After which the automatic recharge will begin.

7

Step 7

After payment, charging will begin, where you will see information about the charging process and you can stop the charging process by pressing the stop button.

8

Step 8

During the charging process, you can keep an eye on the charging process by selecting the charger you are using on the map.

9

Step 9

When we select the desired charger on the map, we will be able to find out information about the connector information.

10

Step 10

Through the profile page, you can change information, monitor and view your payment and recharge history.
